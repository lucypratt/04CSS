Six Strings Music

Welcome to Six Strings Music! Here you will find all kinds of information on teaching and learning new instruments. Whether you’re a new teacher looking for music theory worksheets or a beginner musician striving to improve your skills, Six Strings Music is the right place for you.

Our Learning Resources range from music book suggestions to music theory worksheets. Browse sheet music and links to some of the most popular music libraries on the internet. Don’t forget to check out our recommended curriculum for student skill levels beginner, intermediate, and advanced.

Picking up an instrument can be hard. Especially if you’re doing it on your own without the support of a teacher. Our learning resources are great for understanding music theory and finding new exercises to challenge yourself but developing musical abilities takes time and practice. Read up on our blog for tips to optimize time spent practicing and suggestions to help you have fun on your musical journey. Whether you’re learning as a hobby or for a class, these pointers will have you enjoying your practice and set you well on your way to mastering the instrument.
